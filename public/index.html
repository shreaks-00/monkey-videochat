FAQs

Common questions about using Monkey

What Is Monkey?

Monkey is a free random video chat platform that allows you to meet and talk to strangers from around the world instantly via peer-to-peer connection.

Is Monkey Safe to Use?

Yes, we prioritize simplicity and security. Because video streams connect directly peer-to-peer, your data isn't recorded or stored by our servers. We encourage respectful behavior and you can always click "Next" to instantly end an uncomfortable chat.

Can I Use Monkey to Talk to Strangers?

Absolutely. Monkey is explicitly designed to let you talk to strangers and meet new friends globally in a spontaneous, fun way.

Do I Need an Account to Get Started?

No account is needed! Simply click "Start" and jump right into real face-to-face conversations. No sign-up, no personal data required.

Is Monkey Available on Mobile and Desktop?

Yes, Monkey works seamlessly across all your devices directly in your web browser—no app download required.
